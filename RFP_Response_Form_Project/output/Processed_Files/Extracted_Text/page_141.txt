Personnel positions will be identified as Non-Key Personnel at the Task Order level.
Submission of resumes for Non-Key Personnel positions are not required.

The Government reserves the right to review non-Key resumes prior to
onboarding.

The Government reserves the right to reyect non-Key personnel that do not meet
the minimum requirements listed.
